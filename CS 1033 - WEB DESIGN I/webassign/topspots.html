Top Spots | Western's Campus
 

My favourite spots for others to discover on campus are:

Favourite spot to workout:

The Western Student Recreation Centre is a great place to relieve stress and let off steam. It is very conveniently located on campus and is a great spot to meet up with friends to get a pump. I try to go as much as possible, and lately I have been going almost everyday!

 

 

Favourite spot to eat: 

The spoke is a great way to fill up on a busy day. I like coming to the spoke after workouts especially to pickup a bagel with cream cheese and a protein shake. 

 

  
 

 

Favourite spot to study: 

The D.B. Weldon Library is a massive facility that caters to everyone's study environments. They have places to study for groups and by yourself. I like to go and study by myself to get less distracted!

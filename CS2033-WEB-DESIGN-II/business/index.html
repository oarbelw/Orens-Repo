A little about us

Here at Flowers by Oren, we have been proudly arranging exquisite floral arrangements for you since 2002. Located in London Ontario, our goal is environmental responsibility and sustainable practices when it comes to procuring our flowers. We are dedicated to sourcing locally and ethically grown seasonal flowers. This way, we can promote environmental conservation, support local farmers and reduce the carbon footprint associated with non-local importation. As a result, we can provide our clients with the freshest and most appealing bouquet, tailored to their needs. Come check us out and contribute to a greener world!

Meet the team

Oren Arbel-Wood

Founding Member/CEO

Oren founded this company in 2002, uninspired by the quality of the flower market in London. After graduating from the University of Western Ontario with a bachelors in Computer Science, Oren remained in the area to follow his dream of creating a flower shop. Oren's people skills and experience in software helped grow the company to great heights. Oren works on this full time as the founding member and CEO.

Dalia Piatigorsky-Roth

Founding Member/Environmentalist

Dalia was born and raised in Toronto Ontario and attended school at Dalhousie University where she received her bachelors of sustainability. Subsequently, she heard Oren's desire to create a company and joined in 2002 to start the company. Her passion for the environment and invaluable sustainability skills is what differentiated Flowers by Oren. She works alongside Oren ensuring the sustainable gathering of flowers.

Josh Feldman

Store staff

Josh met Oren at the University of Western Ontario where he was studying for bachelors in business management. After graduating together, Josh travelled the world and gained invaluable insight on many flowers, both common and unique. After Josh returned in 2004, Oren brought Josh on as the florist and store worker. His deep knowledge of flowers attracts many customers from all around Canada.
